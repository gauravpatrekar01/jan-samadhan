How JanSamadhan Works

A streamlined, 4-step process to get your grievance registered and resolved transparently.

1
📝

Register

Fill the structured grievance form with your details, category, and location. Upload supporting images.

→
2
🎯

Auto-Prioritize

Our smart system automatically prioritizes your complaint. Emergencies get instant escalation.

→
3
🧑‍💼

Officer Review

Assigned to the relevant department. Officers take action and update status in real-time.

→
4
✅

Platform Capabilities

Why JanSamadhan?

🎯
Smart Priority Queue

Emergency cases auto-escalate. Our queue ensures critical issues reach officers first.

📊
Real-Time Analytics

Admins and officers get live dashboards with charts, region heatmaps, and KPIs.

🗺️
Location Mapping

JanBot AI Assistant

24/7 chatbot guides citizens through the process, answers FAQs, and provides updates.
