JanSamadhan Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

JanSamadhan is a Smart Public Grievance and Resolution System designed to help citizens report issues and track resolutions efficiently. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, and phone number
  • Government identification (e.g., Aadhaar) for verification (securely handled)
  • Complaint details including description, category, and location
  • Uploaded media (images, videos, documents)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and resolve grievances
  • To verify user identity and prevent misuse
  • To improve system performance and user experience
  • To send notifications regarding complaint status

4. Data Sharing & Access

Your data is only shared with authorized personnel such as government officers and verified NGOs assigned to your complaint. Sensitive information like Aadhaar numbers is never exposed to NGOs.

5. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted and securely stored
  • Strict role-based access control is enforced
  • Regular monitoring and security audits are performed

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

  • You can view and update your profile information
  • You can request deletion of your account
  • You can track all your submitted complaints

8. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve user experience and system performance.

9. Changes to This Policy

JanSamadhan reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
